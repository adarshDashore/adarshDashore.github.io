Super is a subscription service that provides care and repair for your home.

Become a member and we’ll pay to repair or replace covered appliance and home system breakdowns. Super’s concierge service manages the logistics for your home service needs. From your desktop, tablet, or mobile phone, you’re only one tap away from getting the service you need for your home. Play Video

How It Works

Sign up for a Super subscription today. Then, just call, click, or tap next time you have a repair or maintenance need, and we’ll send a top servicer to get it done.

Super helps you coordinate many of your home needs - from maintenance to repair.

Service is available over the phone, online, or via mobile.

We send the best service providers, at the best time for you.

Super repairs or replaces covered appliances and systems when they break.

Make expenses predictable

Pay one low monthly or yearly fee, and Super will repair or replace covered home systems or appliances when they break.

Simplify with one source for services

Take the guesswork out of finding and scheduling help for everything from clearing out your rain gutters to replacing your fridge filter.

Receive best-in-class service

Work with the highest quality service providers carefully evaluated, selected, and monitored by Super.

Enjoy greater buying power

Receive better pricing and availability on services as a member, benefiting from Super’s relationships with service providers.

Prevent problems from happening

Get regular updates on what needs to be done in your home (with the option to have us do it for you).

Have one home record

Keep records of all the service and maintenance work done on your home in one centralized online location.
